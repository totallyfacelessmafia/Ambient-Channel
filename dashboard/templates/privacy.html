Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used for login and account recovery
  • Password — stored as a one-way bcrypt hash (we never see your actual password)
  • Channel data — YouTube channel URLs, channel names, visual preferences, and reference thumbnails you provide during onboarding
  • Generated content — images, videos, and music created through the platform

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your account and provide the Service
  • To generate AI content tailored to your channel style
  • To upload videos to YouTube on your behalf (when you authorize this)
  • To improve the Service and fix bugs

3. Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with:

  • fal.ai — for AI image and music generation
  • YouTube Data API — for channel data fetching and video uploads
  • Google OAuth — for YouTube upload authorization

These services have their own privacy policies. We send only the minimum data needed for each operation.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. We use HTTPS for all connections. We do not sell your data to third parties.

5. YouTube API Services

ChannelForge's use of YouTube API Services is subject to YouTube's Terms of Service and Google's Privacy Policy. You can revoke ChannelForge's access to your YouTube account at any time via your Google Security Settings.

6. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account is active. Generated content (images, videos) may be stored for up to 90 days after creation. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting support.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your generated content at any time

8. Cookies

We use a session cookie to keep you logged in. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or analytics.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via the dashboard.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at the email address provided in your dashboard.
